What is Marvel ?

Comics

Marvel Comics is an American superheroes comic books publisher that was founded 83 years ago, in 1939 by Martin Goodman as Timely Comics that, later in 1951, became Atlas Comics. In 1961 the era of Marvel Comics began with the appearance of The Fantastic Four superhero team which was created by Stan Lee and many others. Marvel owns many characters that became very famous around the world, such as Spider-Mam, Iron Man and superhero teams like Avnegers or X-Men. Many Marvel characters operate in a single reality, known as Marvel Universe, most of their action locations reflecting into real life places, like New York City.

Marvel Cinematic Universe

Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is an American media franchise, and shared universe that is centered on a series of superhero movies, television series, and short films which are produced by Marvel Studios. The movies are based on some comic books published by Marvel Comics.
MCU starts with the 2008 Iron Man movie, which is the first movie in Phase One and directed by Jon Favreau. The last movie released is Thor: Love and Thunder (July 2022).

...but what about DC ?

Comics

DC Comics is also an American superheroes comic books publisher, competing with Marvel Comics. It is a branch of Warner Bros. Discovery and one of the largest and oldest American comic book companies, first comic being published in 1937. The majority of the publications take place in DC Universe and they feature a variety of iconic heroes such as Superman, Batman and Wonder Woman. Justice League and Teen Titans are some of the most recognizable superhero teams in DC Universe. Along with Marvel Comics, DC Comics shared somewhere around of the 70% of the American comics in 2017.

DC Extended Universe

DC Extended Universe (DCEU) is a media franchise that follows the actions that take place in the series and movies produced by DC Films and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. Based on the DC Universe comics, the DCEU starts with the Man of Steel , released in 2013, that is a reboot of the previous Superman film sreies. There are a few feature films: Black Adam , Shazam! Fury of the Gods, The Flash, etc.
The DCEU producers decided to include television series on HBO Max, starting with Peacemaker.
